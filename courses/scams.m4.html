Scams that Broke the Economy

There are many schemes run by fraudsters to part people from their money, but some are so successful that they do serious damage to the whole economy.

Simon Ritchie's talk covers a number of these scams and shows that they all have some common themes, in particular a piece of magic technology that few people understand and which promises unlimited wealth.
